## **Allocator**
Allocator group contains the metrics of allocator. 

---

### _**SegmentID**_

**Type**: Integer

**Monitoring**: Optional

**Introduced**: v0.10.0

The identifier of the segment.

---

### _**SegmentState**_

**Type**: Integer

**Monitoring**: Optional

**Introduced**: v0.10.0

The state of the segment.

---

### _**InvalidBlockCount**_

**Type**: Integer

**Monitoring**: Optional

**Introduced**: v0.10.0

The number of invalid blocks in the segment.

---

### _**TotalSegments**_

**Type**: Integer

**Monitoring**: Optional

**Introduced**: v0.10.0

The total number of segments in the allocator. 

---
